AFTER DOWNLOADING:

1. Make a new folder on your computer
   Example: "spotify-mixer"

2. Put all 5 files in that folder

3. Open Terminal/Command Prompt

4. Go to your folder:
   cd ~/spotify-mixer
   (or wherever you put the files)

5. Run this command:
   bash run.sh
   
   (Windows: python app_enhanced.py, then in another window: python -m http.server 8000)

6. Wait 5 seconds, then open:
   http://localhost:8000

7. That's it! The app opens in your browser!
